About Divinestamp

Precision from
India's Auto Hub

Divinestamp Private Limited is a modern precision aluminium component manufacturing company established in February 2025 in Hosur, Tamil Nadu — one of India's fastest-growing automotive industrial hubs.

We specialize in manufacturing Front Fork Outer Tubes — critical structural components in two-wheeler front suspension systems — using advanced VMC (Vertical Machining Center) technology and high-grade aluminium alloys.

"From raw aluminium to precision-engineered automotive components — every step is driven by engineering excellence and an uncompromising commitment to quality."

PLR
Mr. PLR Reddy
Founder & Managing Director — Divinestamp Pvt Ltd

Mission

To manufacture high-quality aluminium components with precision engineering and customer satisfaction as our highest priority — delivering reliable components that power India's automotive industry.

Vision

To establish Divinestamp as a leading aluminium component manufacturer in India, recognized for engineering excellence, innovation, and trusted OEM partnerships.
